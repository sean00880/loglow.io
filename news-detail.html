What is Our Secret Logo Desing Recipe?

A 'logo' is more than just a square-shaped piece of art. Your brand logo represents everything your brand stands for using an illustration, some text, or both.

Here at Loglow, we take our profession very seriously. For each client, we follow a system which helps ensure that our delivered product meets the highest standards of consistency and qality, and has a transformational impact on your brand.

The first part of the design process involves four of our designers creating initial designs per client requirements. We then simulate the application of logo in action and test for it's appeal to audience. This test is crucial in helping us get a clear idea on what emotion these logos convey. Then we shortlist two of the best logos and create more variations of them with multiple color schemes and present them to the client.

By incoroperating multiple desingers in each of our logos, we are able to create and deliver logos that are aesthetically pleasing and powerful. These logos will help convey your message to a broader range of audience and spark curiosity amongst the masses.

You also get multiple logo designs to chose from before you make your final decision, and then our team finalizes the desgin and delivers to you overall the most powerful and creative logo you could ask for.
